SankalpTV, a media house founded by Ashok Das, a senior journalist with 40+ years of experience is based in Bhubaneswar. Since its inception in 1998, SankalpTV has produced more than 4000 programs of different genres. It has its own studio 'Sankalp Media & Convention' at Infocity Area, Bhubaneswar with required production and post-production facilities as well as experienced human resources. Some of the most loved programs produced by SankalpTV are: - Odisha Diary (Aired on DD Odia from 1999-2004) - Spandan (Aired on DD Odia from 2003-2003) - Aashara Aloka (Aired on ETV from 2006-2015) - Bhinna Manisa (Aired on DD Odia from 2006-2008) - Sunara Odisha (Aired on OTV from 2008-2014) - Aama School dot com (Aired on DD Odia from 2014-2014) - Aama Chaasa (Aired on DD Odia from 2020-present) At present, SankalpTV is more focused on free dissemination of contents through mainstream as well as social media in the field of agriculture & allied activities, skill development, education and livelihoods, etc. for empowering lives of many. We believe that with the penetration of smartphones, digital media can truly be a tool for development and our journey can be more meaningful with the cooperation of like-minded persons/organizations.
